
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

#### PART 547 - DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 547.313 Financial, material, logistical, or technological support.
####### financial, material, logistical, or technological support,

The termas used in ยง 547.201(a)(2)(vi), means any property, tangible or intangible, including currency, financial instruments, securities, or any other transmission of value; weapons or related materiel; chemical or biological agents; explosives; false documentation or identification; communications equipment; computers; electronic or other devices or equipment; technologies; lodging; safe houses; facilities; vehicles or other means of transportation; or goods. "Technologies" as used in this definition means specific information necessary for the development, production, or use of a product, including related technical data such as blueprints, plans, diagrams, models, formulae, tables, engineering designs and specifications, manuals, or other recorded instructions.
