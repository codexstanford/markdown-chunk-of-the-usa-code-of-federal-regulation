
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

###### ยง 570.304 Financial, material, logistical, or technical support; financial, material, logistical, or technological support.

The terms financial, material, logistical, or technical support, and financial, material, logistical, or technological support, as used, respectively, in ยง 570.201(a)(3)(iv) and ยง 570.201(a)(4)(vi), mean any property, tangible or intangible, including currency, financial instruments, securities, or any other transmission of value; weapons or related materiel; chemical or biological agents; explosives; false documentation or identification; communications equipment; computers; electronic or other devices or equipment; technologies; lodging; safe houses; facilities; vehicles or other means of transportation; or goods.
