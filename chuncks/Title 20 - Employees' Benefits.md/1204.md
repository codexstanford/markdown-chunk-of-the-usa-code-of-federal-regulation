
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

#### PART 220 - DETERMINING DISABILITY
##### Subpart D - Disability Under the Railroad Retirement Act for Any Regular Employment
###### ยง 220.27 What is needed to show an impairment.

A physical or mental impairment must result from anatomical, physiological, or psychological abnormalities which can be shown by medically acceptable clinical and laboratory diagnostic techniques. A physical or mental impairment must be established by medical evidence consisting of signs, symptoms, and laboratory findings, not only by the claimant's statement of symptoms.
