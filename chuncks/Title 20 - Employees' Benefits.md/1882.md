###### ยง 234.11 1974 Act lump-sum death payment.

(a) The total amount of the 1974 Act LSDP is payable to the employee's widow(er), if she or he was "living in the same household" as the employee at the time of the employee's death. (Refer to ยง 234.21 for an explanation of "living in the same household.")
