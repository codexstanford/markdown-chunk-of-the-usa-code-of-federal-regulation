##### ยง 679.120 What is meant by the terms "optimum policy-making authority" and "demonstrated experience and expertise"?

For purposes of ยง 679.110:

(b) A representative with "demonstrated experience and expertise" means an individual with documented leadership in developing or implementing workforce development, human resources, training and development, or a core program function.
