
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter B - Procedural Rules

##### Subpart D - Special Rules Applicable to Proceedings Initiated by the FAA
###### ยง 16.103 Notice of investigation.

Following the initiation of an investigation under ยง 16.101, the FAA sends a notice to the person(s) subject to investigation.
