(a) Each fuel tank filler connection must prevent the entrance of fuel into any part of the rotorcraft other than the tank itself during normal operations and must be crash resistant during a survivable impact in accordance with ยง 29.952(c). In addition-

(1) Each filler must be marked as prescribed in ยง 29.1557(c)(1);
