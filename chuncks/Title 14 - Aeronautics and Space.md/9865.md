### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 259 - ENHANCED PROTECTIONS FOR AIRLINE PASSENGERS
##### ยง 259.3 Definitions.
###### Covered carrier

means a certificated carrier, a commuter carrier, or a foreign air carrier operating to, from or within the United States, conducting scheduled passenger service or public charter service with at least one aircraft having a designed seating capacity of 30 or more seats.
