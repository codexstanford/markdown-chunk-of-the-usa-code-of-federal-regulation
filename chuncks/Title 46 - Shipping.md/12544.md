
# Title 46 - Shipping

##### Subpart C - Additional Requirements for Passenger Vessels
###### ยง 199.240 Muster stations.

Each passenger vessel must, in addition to meeting the requirements of ยง 199.110, have muster stations that-

(a) Are near the embarkation stations, unless a muster station is also an embarkation station;
