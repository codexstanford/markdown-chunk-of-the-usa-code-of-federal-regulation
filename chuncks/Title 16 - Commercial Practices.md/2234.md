
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter D - Trade Regulation Rules
#### PART 453 - FUNERAL INDUSTRY PRACTICES
##### ยง 453.8 Declaration of intent.

(a) Except as otherwise provided in ยง 453.2(a), it is a violation of this rule to engage in any unfair or deceptive acts or practices specified in this rule, or to fail to comply with any of the preventive requirements specified in this rule;
