### Subchapter A - General

##### Subpart C - Operation of Advisory Committees
###### ยง 1018.24 Agenda.

Prior to each advisory committee meeting, the Advisory Committee Management Officer shall prepare and, after approval by the officer or employee designated under ยง 1018.23 (a), shall distribute to each committee member the agenda for that meeting.
