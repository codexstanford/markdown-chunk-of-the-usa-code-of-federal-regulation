
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter B - Consumer Product Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1201 - SAFETY STANDARD FOR ARCHITECTURAL GLAZING MATERIALS
##### Subpart A - The Standard
###### § 1201.4 Test procedures.
####### American National Standard for Safety Glazing Materials Used in Building-Safety Performance Specifications and Methods of Test,",http://www.archives.gov/federal-register/cfr/ibr-locations.html.
