###### Subpart C - Responsibilities of NEH Recipients
####### ยง 1172.23 Self-evaluation.

As part of a compliance review under ยง 1172.31 or a complaint investigation under ยง 1172.34, NEH may require a recipient employing the equivalent of fifteen (15) or more full time employees to complete a written self-evaluation, in a manner specified by NEH, of any age distinction imposed in its program or activity receiving Federal financial assistance.
