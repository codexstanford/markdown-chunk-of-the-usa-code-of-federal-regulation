##### Subpart E - Discrimination on the Basis of Sex in Employment in Education Programs or Activities Prohibited

####### Pregnancy as a temporary disability.

(c)A recipient shall treat pregnancy, childbirth, false pregnancy, termination of pregnancy, and recovery therefrom and any temporary disability resulting therefrom as any other temporary disability for all job related purposes, including commencement, duration and extensions of leave, payment of disability income, accrual of seniority and any other benefit or service, and reinstatement, and under any fringe benefit offered to employees by virtue of employment.
