
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### Subpart F - Federal Civil Penalties
###### ยง 158.603 Notice to responsible entities.

If HHS learns of a potential violation described in ยง 158.602 of this subpart or if a State informs HHS of a potential violation prior to imposing any civil monetary penalty HHS must provide written notice to the issuer, to include the following:

(a) Describe the potential violation.
