
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### ยง 1606.12 Time and waiver.

(a) Except for the 6-year time limit for debarments in ยง 1606.11(d) of this part, any period of time provided in these rules may, upon good cause shown and determined, be extended in writing:

(1) By the Corporation, unless a hearing officer has been appointed;
