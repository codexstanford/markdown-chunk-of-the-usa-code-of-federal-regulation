
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

##### Import Requirements
###### ยง 10.587 Maintenance of records.
####### General.

(a)An importer claiming preferential tariff treatment for a good imported into the United States under ยง 10.583(b) of this subpart must maintain, for a minimum of five years after the date of importation of the good, all records and documents that the importer has demonstrating that the good qualifies for preferential tariff treatment under the CAFTA-DR.
