
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

### PART 10 - ARTICLES CONDITIONALLY FREE, SUBJECT TO A REDUCED RATE, ETC.
#### Subpart P - United States-Oman Free Trade Agreement
##### Rules of Origin
###### ยง 10.872 Definitions.
####### Remanufactured good.

(m)"Remanufactured good" means an industrial good that is assembled in the territory of a Party and that:

(1) Is entirely or partially comprised of recovered goods;

(2) Has a similar life expectancy to a like good that is new; and

(3) Enjoys the factory warranty similar to that of a like good that is new;
