##### Subpart C - Disclosure of Ownership and Control Information
###### ยง 420.201 Definitions.
####### Indirect ownership interest

means any ownership interest in an entity that has an ownership interest in the disclosing entity. The term includes an ownership interest in any entity that has an indirect ownership interest in the disclosing entity.
