###### ยง 17.99 Critical habitat; plants on the Hawaiian Islands.
####### Clermontia lindseyana

Maui-Montane Mesic-Unit 1, identified in the legal descriptions in paragraph (e)(1) of this section, constitutes critical habitat foron Maui. In unit Maui-Montane Mesic-Unit 1, the physical and biological features of critical habitat are:

(i) Elevation: 3,300 to 6,500 ft (1,000 to 2,000 m).

(ii) Annual precipitation: 50 to 75 in (130 to 190 cm).

(ii) Substrate: Deep ash deposits, thin silty loams.
