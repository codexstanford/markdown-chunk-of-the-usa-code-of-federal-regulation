
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1919 - GEAR CERTIFICATION
##### Subpart E - Certification of Vessels: Tests and Proof Loads; Heat Treatment; Competent Persons
###### ยง 1919.33 Proof tests - wire rope.

Wire rope, except as provided in ยง 1919.14(b), shall be tested by sample, a piece being tested to destruction, and the safe working load of running ropes, unless otherwise acceptable to the Administration on the basis of design, shall not exceed one-fifth of the breaking load of the sample tested.
