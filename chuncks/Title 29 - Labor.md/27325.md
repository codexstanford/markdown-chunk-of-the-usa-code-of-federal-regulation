
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XXVII - Federal Mine Safety and Health Review Commission
#### PART 2700 - PROCEDURAL RULES
##### Subpart C - Contests of Proposed Penalties
###### ยง 2700.26 Notice of contest of proposed penalty assessment.

A person has 30 days after receipt of the proposed penalty assessment within which to notify the Secretary that he contests the proposed penalty assessment. A person who wishes to contest a proposed penalty assessment must provide such notification regardless of whether the person has previously contested the underlying citation or order pursuant to ยง 2700.20. The Secretary shall immediately transmit to the Commission any notice of contest of a proposed penalty assessment.
