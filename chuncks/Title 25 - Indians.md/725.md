
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter D - Human Services
#### PART 26 - JOB PLACEMENT AND TRAINING PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Applicability
###### ยง 26.1 What terms do I need to know?
####### On or Near Reservation

means those areas or communities adjacent or contiguous to reservations, or service areas where Job Training and Placement programs are provided upon approval of the Assistant Secretary-Indian Affairs or his designated representative. For purposes of this program and services, Alaska is included in this definition.
