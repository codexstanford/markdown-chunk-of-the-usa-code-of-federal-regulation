
# Title 25 - Indians

##### ยง 900.24 Can a contract proposal for an Indian tribe or tribal organization's share of administrative programs, functions, services, and activities be declined for any reason other than the five reasons specified in ยง 900.22?

No. The Secretary may only decline a proposal based upon one or more of the five reasons listed above.
