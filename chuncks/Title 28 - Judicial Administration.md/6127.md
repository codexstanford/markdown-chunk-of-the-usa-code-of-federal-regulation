
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter III - Federal Prison Industries, Inc., Department of Justice
### PART 345 - FEDERAL PRISON INDUSTRIES (FPI) INMATE WORK PROGRAMS
#### Subpart D - Recruitment and Hiring Practices
##### ยง 345.32 Hiring.

(a) Inmate workers are ordinarily hired through waiting lists. Except as noted in ยง 345.33, inmates are to be placed on the waiting lists in order of receipt of applications for work with Industries, and are to be hired in the same sequence.
