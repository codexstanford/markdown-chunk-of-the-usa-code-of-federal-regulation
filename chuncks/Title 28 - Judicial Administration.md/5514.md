###### ยง 94.22 Categories of expenses.

The following categories of expenses, generally, may be reimbursed, with some limitations, as noted in ยง 94.23: medical care; mental health care; property loss; funeral and burial; and miscellaneous expenses (including temporary lodging, emergency travel, and transportation).
