####### Timing requirements for contemporaneous purchase credit.

(3)When credit under an open-end credit plan is granted, extended, or provided to a consumer in person or by telephone for the purpose of financing the contemporaneous purchase of goods or services, any risk-based pricing notice required to be provided pursuant to this subpart (or the disclosures permitted under ยง 222.74(e) or (f)) may be provided at the earlier of:

(ii) Within 30 days after the decision to approve the grant, extension, or other provision of credit.
