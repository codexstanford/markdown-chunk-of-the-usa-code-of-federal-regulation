
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart F - Civil Service Retirement and Disability Fund Offset
###### ยง 313.142 Request for administrative offset.

The Director shall request administrative offset under ยง 313.140, as soon as practical after completion of the applicable procedures in order to help ensure that offset be initiated prior to expiration of the applicable statute of limitations.
