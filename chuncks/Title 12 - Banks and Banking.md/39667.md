
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Examples.

7.Examples of disclosures that could be used to comply with the requirements of § 1026.16(h)(3) include: "no interest if paid in full within 6 months" and "no interest if paid in full by December 31, 2010."
