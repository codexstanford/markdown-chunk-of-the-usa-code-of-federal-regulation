(a) If, in the Administrator's judgment, an alternative means of emission limitation will achieve a reduction in VOC emissions at least equivalent to the reduction in VOC emissions achieved by the applicable requirement in ยง 60.692, the Administrator will publish in thea notice permitting the use of the alternative means for purposes of compliance with that requirement.
