
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 96 - NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM AND CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS FOR STATE IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
####### ยง 96.2 Definitions.
######## NO,authorized account representative

means, for a NOBudget source or NOBudget unit at the source, the natural person who is authorized by the owners and operators of the source and all NOBudget units at the source, in accordance with subpart B of this part, to represent and legally bind each owner and operator in matters pertaining to the NOBudget Trading Program or, for a general account, the natural person who is authorized, in accordance with subpart F of this part, to transfer or otherwise dispose of NOallowances held in the general account.
