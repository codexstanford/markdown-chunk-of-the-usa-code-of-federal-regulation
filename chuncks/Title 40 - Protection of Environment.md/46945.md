If engine stalling should occur during cycle operation, follow the provisions of ยง 86.136-90 (engine starting and restarting). For gasoline-fueled Otto-cycle vehicles, the composite samples collected in bags are analyzed for THC, CO, CO, CH, and NO. For petroleum-fueled diesel-cycle vehicles, THC is sampled and analyzed continuously according to the provisions of ยง 86.110. Parallel bag samples of dilution air are analyzed for THC, CO, CO, CH, and NO.
