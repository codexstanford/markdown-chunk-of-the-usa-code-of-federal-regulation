######## Baseline NO,emission rate.

(d)The unit's baseline NOemission rate shall equal:

(1) If the unit's NOemissions rate and heat input are monitored and reported for only one control period, in accordance with paragraph (b)(1) of this section, the unit's NOemissions rate (in lb/mmBtu) for the control period;

(2) If the unit's NOemissions rate and heat input are monitored and reported for more than one control period, in accordance with paragraphs (b)(1) and (2) of this section, and the unit does not have add-on NOemission controls during any such control periods, the average of the amounts of the unit's NOemissions rate (in lb/mmBtu) for the control periods under paragraphs (b)(1)(ii) and (2) of this section; or

(3) If the unit's NOemissions rate and heat input are monitored and reported for more than one control period, in accordance with paragraphs (b)(1) and (2) of this section, and the unit has add-on NOemission controls during any such control periods, the average of the amounts of the unit's NOemissions rate (in lb/mmBtu) for such control periods during which the unit has add-on NOemission controls.
