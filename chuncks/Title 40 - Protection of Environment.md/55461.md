
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 135 - PRIOR NOTICE OF CITIZEN SUITS
##### Subpart B - Prior Notice Under the Safe Drinking Water Act
###### ยง 135.12 Contents of notice.
####### Failure to act.

(b)Notice regarding an alleged failure of the Administrator to perform any act or duty under the Act which is not discretionary with the Administrator shall identify the provision of the Act which requires the act or creates the duty, and shall describe with reasonable specificity the action taken or not taken by the Administrator which is alleged to constitute a failure to perform such act or duty, and shall state the full name, address, and telephone number of the person giving notice.
