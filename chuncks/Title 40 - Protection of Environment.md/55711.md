##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 141.2 Definitions.
####### Sanitary survey

means an onsite review of the water source, facilities, equipment, operation and maintenance of a public water system for the purpose of evaluating the adequacy of such source, facilities, equipment, operation and maintenance for producing and distributing safe drinking water.
