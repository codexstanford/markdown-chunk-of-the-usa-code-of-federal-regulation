
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### Model Rule - Initial Compliance Requirements
####### ยง 60.5195 By what date must I conduct the initial air pollution control device inspection and make any necessary repairs?

(a) You must conduct an air pollution control device inspection according to ยง 60.5220(c) by the final compliance date under the approved state plan, Federal plan, or delegation, as applicable.
