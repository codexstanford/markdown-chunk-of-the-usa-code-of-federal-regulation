
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter B - Grants and Other Federal Assistance

###### General
####### ยง 35.6015 Definitions.
######## Cooperative Agreement.

A legal instrument EPA uses to transfer money, property, services, or anything of value to a recipient to accomplish a public purpose in which substantial EPA involvement is anticipated during the performance of the project.
