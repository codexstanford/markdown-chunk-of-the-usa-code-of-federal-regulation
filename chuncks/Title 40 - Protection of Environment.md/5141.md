
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency

##### Subpart I - Review of New Sources and Modifications

####### Electric utility steam generating unit

(xx)means any steam electric generating unit that is constructed for the purpose of supplying more than one-third of its potential electric output capacity and more than 25 MW electrical output to any utility power distribution system for sale. Any steam supplied to a steam distribution system for the purpose of providing steam to a steam-electric generator that would produce electrical energy for sale is also considered in determining the electrical energy output capacity of the affected facility.
