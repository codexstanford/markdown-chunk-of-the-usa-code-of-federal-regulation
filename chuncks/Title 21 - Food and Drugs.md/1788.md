(b) If the Commissioner, after a regulatory hearing or after the time for requesting a hearing expires without a request being made, upon an evaluation of the administrative record of the disqualification proceeding, does not make the findings required in ยง 58.202, he shall issue a final order terminating the disqualification proceeding.
