
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to National Defense
### Chapter XVIII - National Counterintelligence Center
#### PART 1802 - CHALLENGES TO CLASSIFICATION OF DOCUMENTS BY AUTHORIZED HOLDERS PURSUANT TO SECTION 1.9 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER 12958
##### Subpart B - Filing Of Challenges
###### ยง 1802.14 Transmission.

Authorized holders must direct challenge requests to NACIC as specified in ยง 1802.3. The classified nature of the challenge, as well as the appended documents, require that the holder transmit same in full accordance with established security procedures. In general, registered U.S. mail is approved for SECRET, non-compartmented material; higher classifications require use of approved Top Secret facsimile machines or NACIC-approved couriers. Further information is available from NACIC as well as corporate or other federal agency security departments.
