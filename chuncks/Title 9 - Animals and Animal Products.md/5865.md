
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products
## Chapter III - Food Safety and Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter A - Agency Organization and Terminology; Mandatory Meat and Poultry Products Inspection and Voluntary Inspection and Certification
#### PART 318 - ENTRY INTO OFFICIAL ESTABLISHMENTS; REINSPECTION AND PREPARATION OF PRODUCTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 318.18 Handling of certain material for mechanical processing.

Material to be processed into "Mechanically Separated (Species)" shall be so processed within 1 hour from the time it is cut or separated from carcasses or parts of carcasses, except that such product may be held for no more than 72 hours at 40 &#xB0;F. (4 &#xB0;C.) or less, or held indefinitely at 0 &#xB0;F. (-18 &#xB0;C.) or less. "Mechanically Separated (Species)" shall, directly after being processed, be used as an ingredient in a meat food product except that it may be held prior to such use for no more than 72 hours at 40 &#xB0;F. (4 &#xB0;C.) or less or indefinitely at 0 &#xB0;F. (-18 &#xB0;C.) or less.
