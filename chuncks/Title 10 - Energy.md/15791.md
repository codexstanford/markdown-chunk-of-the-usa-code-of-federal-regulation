
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 862 - RESTRICTIONS ON AIRCRAFT LANDING AND AIR DELIVERY AT DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY NUCLEAR SITES
#### ยง 862.1 Purpose.
##### et seq.

The purpose of this part is to set forth Department of Energy, hereinafter "DOE", security policy regarding aircraft and air delivery on nuclear sites under the jurisdiction of DOE pursuant to the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended (42 U.S.C. 2011).
