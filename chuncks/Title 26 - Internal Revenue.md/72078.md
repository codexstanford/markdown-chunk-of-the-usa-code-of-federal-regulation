####### ยง 301.6058-2 Required use of electronic form for filing requirements relating to information required in connection with certain plans of deferred compensation.
######## Electronic-filing requirements under section 6058.,IRS.gov

(a)A return required under section 6058 with respect to an employee benefit plan must be filed electronically if the filer is required by the Internal Revenue Code or regulations to file at least 10 returns during the calendar year that includes the first day of the plan year. The Commissioner may direct the type of electronic filing and may also exempt certain returns from the electronic requirements of this section through revenue procedures, publications, forms, instructions, or other guidance, including postings on thewebsite. Returns filed electronically must be made in accordance with the applicable revenue procedures, publications, forms, instructions, or other guidance.
