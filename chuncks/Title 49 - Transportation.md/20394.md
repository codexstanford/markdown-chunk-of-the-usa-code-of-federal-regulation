
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 570 - VEHICLE IN USE INSPECTION STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - Vehicles With GVWR of 10,000 Pounds or Less
###### ยง 570.5 Service brake system.
####### Brake system integrity.

(b)The brake system shall demonstrate integrity as indicated by no perceptible decrease in pedal height under a 125 pound force applied to the brake pedal or by no illumination of the brake system failure indicator lamp. The brake system shall withstand the application of force to the pedal without failure of any line or other part.
