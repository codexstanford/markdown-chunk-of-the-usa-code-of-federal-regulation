###### ยง 1182.8 Miscellaneous requirements.

(a) If applicants wish to withdraw an application, they shall jointly request dismissal in writing.

(b) All pleadings (including motions and replies) submitted under this part shall be served on all other parties, concurrently and by the same (or more expeditious) means with which they are filed with the Board.

(c) Each pleading shall contain a certificate of service stating that the pleading has been served in accordance with paragraph (c) of this section.
