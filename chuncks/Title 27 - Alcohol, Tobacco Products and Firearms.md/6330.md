###### ยง 41.190 Persons required to qualify.

Any person who engages in the business as an importer of tobacco products must qualify as an importer of tobacco products in accordance with this part. Any person eligible for an exemption described in ยง 41.50 is not engaged in the business as an importer of tobacco products.
