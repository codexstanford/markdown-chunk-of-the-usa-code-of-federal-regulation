
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter M - International Traffic in Arms Regulations
#### PART 130 - POLITICAL CONTRIBUTIONS, FEES AND COMMISSIONS
##### ยง 130.7 Supplier.
###### Supplier

means any person who enters into a contract with the Department of Defense for the sale of defense articles or defense services valued in an amount of $500,000 or more under section 22 of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2762).
