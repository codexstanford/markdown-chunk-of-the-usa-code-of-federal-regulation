
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Insurance and Hazard Mitigation

##### Mapping Deficiencies Unrelated to Community-Wide Elevation Determinations
###### ยง 70.4 Review by the Administrator.

The Administrator, after reviewing the scientific or technical information submitted under the provisions of ยง 70.3, shall notify the applicant in writing of his/her determination within 60 days after we receive the applicant's scientific or technical information that we have compared either the ground elevations of an entire legally defined parcel of land or the elevation of the lowest adjacent grade to a structure with the elevation of the base flood and that:

(d) We need an additional 60 days to make a determination.
