
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle F - Federal Travel Regulation System
### Chapter 304 - Payment of Travel Expenses from a Non-Federal Source
#### Subchapter A - Employee's Acceptance of Payment from a Non-Federal Source for Travel Expenses
##### PART 304-3 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITY
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 304-3.4 What payments may my agency or I accept from a non-Federal source?

You or your agency may accept payments other than cash from a non-Federal source for all of your official travel expenses to attend a meeting of mutual interest, or any portion of those travel expenses mutually agreed upon between your agency and the non-Federal source. You may not accept payments for travel that is not to attend a meeting under this part. However, you may be able to accept payments under other authorities (see ยง 304-3.19).
