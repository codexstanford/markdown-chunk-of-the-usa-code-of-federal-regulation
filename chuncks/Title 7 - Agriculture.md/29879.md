####### ยง 1412.31 PLC yields for covered commodities.

(a) Except for seed cotton PLC yield for covered commodities on the farm is equal to the counter-cyclical payment yield established for each covered commodity on the farm that was effective September 30, 2013, unless the PLC yield is updated as specified in ยง 1421.32.
