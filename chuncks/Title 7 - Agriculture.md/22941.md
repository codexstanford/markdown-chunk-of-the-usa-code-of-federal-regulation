
# Title 7 - Agriculture

###### ยง 900.40 Written testimony and USDA data request requirements.

In addition to the provisions of ยง 900.8(b)(4), witnesses at an amendatory fruit, vegetable or nut formal rulemaking hearing shall make, to the extent practicable, at least 8 copies of their testimony, if prepared as an exhibit, and any other exhibits available to USDA before testimony is given on the day of appearance at the hearing.
